WHAT IS CRIC.MAP?

Cric.Map is a simple and affordable tool for the modern cricket batsmen who wants to maximize their efforts while training. Cric.Map TAPE physically marks every connection the ball makes with the bat, giving the batsman instantaneous feedback on where they are hitting the ball on the bat. This feedback affords the batsman the ability to make micro adjustments as they play to proactively improve their form and technique!

Here at Cric.Map we want the world of Cricket and all of its participants to reach their full potential and we sincerely believe that our product can help any Batsman do just that!

OUR STORY

Cric.map's story started with our founder, Uddeshya Singh. He had started out playing cricket for school, as a batsman. However, after a few years of playing he found that he was not improving anymore due to the lack of instant feedback he was getting while practicing. Because of this, he switched to bowling.

Many years later, he is in the Technical Entrepreneurship Master's program and he is thinking about his experience playing cricket to see how he can improve the game. Alongside fellow classmates Adam Weiner and Willem Ytsma, Cric.Map TAPE is born. A simple, inexpensive way to track where the ball is hitting the bat so you can improve your batting form in real time.
