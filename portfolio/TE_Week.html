Engineering Projects: Applicaion of Microfluidic Separation

TE Week

TE Week is the first week of the spring semester of the Technical Entrepreneurship Master's Program at Lehigh University. The goal of TE week is to take a faculty invention that has been patented, find an application for it through customer research, work with the university to draft a mock lisence agreement, and develop a business plan for the venture, all within one week. I was part of a group of four master's students, along with our faculty advisor.

Our Project

The technology that we had to work with was a new microfluidic seperator for condensing nanoparticles for testing. Once we had a solid grasp of the technology and what it did, we began applying creativity techniques to come up with different applications for it.
