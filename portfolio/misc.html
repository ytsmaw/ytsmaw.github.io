Infrared Camera

The summer before my sophomore year of undergrad I decided to make an infrared camera just to see what that would be like. I took apart an old point-and-shoot of mine, replaced the infrared filter with a piece of exposed film (completely black to everything but infrared light), and put it back together. I covered the flash with pieces of exposed film as well to make an invisible, infrared flash.

When I got a new mirrorless camera I took apart my old one and gave it the same treatment. Except this time I didn't cover the sensor with exposed film, so that I could pick up visible as well as infrared light. I made this so that I could take natural-light photographs in locations without much visible light and still get an image. I also did it so that I could pair it with an infrared flash.
